# AIB-AXYS Africa - Privacy Policy

**Last Updated**: January 19, 2026

## 1. Introduction

AIB-AXYS Africa Limited ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy and personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information in compliance with:

- Kenya Data Protection Act, 2019
- GDPR (for EU residents)
- Capital Markets Authority (CMA) regulations

## 2. Data We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide
- **Contact Forms**: Name, email, phone number, CDSC number (optional), messages
- **Account Applications**: Via third-party platforms (not stored by us)
- **Research Downloads**: Email (if required), download tracking

### 2.2 Automatically Collected
- **Technical Data**: IP address, browser type, device information
- **Cookies**: Session cookies for admin authentication, preference cookies for theme
- **Analytics**: Page views, navigation patterns (when Google Analytics is enabled)

## 3. How We Use Your Data

We use your personal data to:
- Respond to inquiries and provide customer support
- Process IPO and leverage applications
- Send research reports (if subscribed)
- Improve our website and services
- Comply with legal and regulatory obligations

**Legal Basis**: 
- Consent (contact forms)
- Legitimate interest (customer service)
- Legal obligation (CMA compliance)

## 4. Data Storage and Security

### 4.1 Where We Store Data
- **Database**: MongoDB hosted securely
- **Files**: Research PDFs stored on server with access controls
- **Location**: Data stored in secure data centers

### 4.2 Security Measures
- Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS)
- Password hashing (bcrypt)
- Access control (role-based permissions)
- Regular security audits
- Secure admin authentication (JWT tokens)

### 4.3 Data Retention
- **Contact submissions**: Retained for 2 years or until deletion requested
- **Audit logs**: Retained for 7 years (regulatory requirement)
- **Admin accounts**: Retained while active

## 5. Your GDPR Rights

You have the right to:

### 5.1 Right to Access
Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you.
**How**: Email info@aib-axysafrica.com with subject "Data Access Request"

### 5.2 Right to Rectification
Correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
**How**: Contact us with correct information

### 5.3 Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten")
Request deletion of your personal data.
**How**: Email info@aib-axysafrica.com with subject "Data Deletion Request"

### 5.4 Right to Data Portability
Receive your data in a machine-readable format.
**How**: Email info@aib-axysafrica.com with subject "Data Export Request"

### 5.5 Right to Object
Object to processing of your personal data.
**How**: Email info@aib-axysafrica.com

### 5.6 Right to Withdraw Consent
Withdraw consent at any time.
**How**: Contact us or use unsubscribe links

## 6. Data Sharing

### 6.1 We DO NOT sell your data.

### 6.2 We may share data with:
- **AXYS Group entities**: For group-wide services
- **Regulatory authorities**: CMA, NSE (when legally required)
- **Service providers**: Email service, hosting (under strict confidentiality)
- **Legal requirement**: Court orders, law enforcement

### 6.3 International Transfers
If data is transferred outside Kenya, we ensure adequate protection through:
- Standard Contractual Clauses
- Adequacy decisions
- Explicit consent
